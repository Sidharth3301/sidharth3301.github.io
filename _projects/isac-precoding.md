This project was my Bachelor's thesis at the Signal and Information Processing Lab, Indian Institute of Science, under the supervision of [Prof. Sundeep Chepuri](https://ece.iisc.ac.in/~spchepuri/).

We studied integrated sensing and communication systems, where a wireless transmitter must serve communication users while also illuminating sensing targets. I co-developed an unsupervised neural precoder that learns to optimize sensing and communication objectives end-to-end without labelled data.

The resulting model improved sensing target illumination while maintaining user SINR constraints, and generalized to user distributions significantly larger than those seen during training.
